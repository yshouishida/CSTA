Welcome to Colegio de Sta. Teresa de Avila
Colegio de Sta. Teresa de Avila is a private school in Novaliches, Quezon City, Philippines. It is a young school that started in 2007, and its establishment was a response to the need for skills training in the community. The school offers a ladderized education, where students take up Technical Vocational Education and Training (TVET) programs that will lead to a college degree later on.

The school offers various programs, including bachelorâ€™s degrees in Elementary Education, Secondary Education, Hotel and Restaurant Management, and Information Technology. It also offers TESDA-accredited courses in Caregiving, Bartending, Bread and Pastry Production, Cookery, Food and Beverage Services, Housekeeping, and Hotel and Restaurant Services.

The school has a relatively good performance in the board exams, with a passing rate of 42% for Elementary Education and 56% for Secondary Education. The school also has a tuition fee range of around P 11,000 to P 27,000 per year, depending on the program.

Overall, Colegio de Sta. Teresa de Avila Foundation is a private school that offers a range of academic and vocational programs to students in the Philippines.
