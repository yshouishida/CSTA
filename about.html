Our School

Vision

CSTA envisions a citizenry whose physical, moral, and intellectual endowments have been harmoniously developed so that its members may gradually acquire a mature sense of responsibility while building their own lives and pursuing true freedom as they face the vicissitudes of life with courage and constancy.

Mission

CSTA, therefore commits itself to the attainment of a true education geared towards the formation of the human person in the puruit of his ultimate end for the good of the society of which, as man, he is a member, and in whose obligation as an adult, he will share.
